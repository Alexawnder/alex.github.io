Cooking Food!

A big hobby that has taken over my life in my recent years has been cooking. It is something that I have grown to love to do whenever I get the chance to. It brings me a lot of joy when I am able to cook for my friends and family and they are able to enjoy the meals that I create. I have grown from someone who has no knowledge of how to cook the most basic things to becoming a normal homecook. I was greatly inspired through watching many cooking videos from people such as Gordon Ramsey, Joshua Weissman, Uncle Roger, Maangchi, and many more great chefs.
